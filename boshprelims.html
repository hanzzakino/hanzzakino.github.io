PART 3 - ESSAY/EXPLANATION
Answer the following questions in your own words. You have the freedom to express yourself here in this part
Tagalog language is allowed.
In your own opinion and based on our discussions, why there is a need to study Basic Occupational Safety and Health (BOSH)? *
There is a motto in every workplaces: 'Safety First'. Our safety and all the people around us in an environment is the most important thing when we are working. BOSH help us understand all the things that we have to keep in mind to be safe in our occupation and to prevent harm not only to ourselves but also to our co-workers. Prevention minimizes all the possible risk that we can face.
Differentiate risk and hazard and cite examples of those terms so that it explains further the difference. *
Risk is the possibility of harm to happen. While hazard is the harm itself. We can to of it this: It is a risk to touch an unknown open wire and the hazard there is getting electrocuted.
What is risk assessment? How can this practice/activity help the people in your workplace? *
Risk assessment is the systematic identification of all the possible hazard that can happen in an environment. It is very important since there is a saying that 'An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure'. It is better to prevent a hazard to happen than to find a cure when it happened.
